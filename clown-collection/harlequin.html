Harlequins



Harlequins are a mixed breed between a jester and a hellound. Their mixed genetics of the entertaining nature of jesters and the chaotic and animalistic demeanor of hellhounds makes them a very difficult breed to take care and is not advised for first-timers.

Diet

Harlequins are a carnivorous breed and rather than aquiring loads of candy like the party clown or fruity foods like the jesters, they need to fresh meat. As fresh as it gets. Harlequin owners will have to ensure that there is enough live meat for their pets to hunt down which may be a big expense.

Housing and Temperament

Adding onto the cost of owning a harlequin, their housing requirments are vastly different from other clowns. These clowns require a mansion in order to live, making them a pet for the elites.
