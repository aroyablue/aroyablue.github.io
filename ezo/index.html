Ezo! The Melting Pot of Asia!




Welcome to Ezo! A newly-developed slice of good food. We are a restaurant primarily focusing on Asian cuisines such as Japanese, Vietnamese and Thai dishes. Here at Ezo’s Kitchen & Sushi Bar, we pride ourselves on the quality of our food, but that’s not all we care about.

Micheal, the founder of Ezo, believes that you can’t fully enjoy the food uniless you understand the food. Generally that means by practicing our cooking skills to make it perfect. But Micheal sees it on another level. He strives to ensure that not only do our customers get exceptional food, but also educate them on Asian culture to help them better enjoy the food.


With that being said, come on in and emerge yourself in delicious foods, as well as learning about the cultures they originate from.
